Gain Voting Power​

In order to place a proposal, every registered delegate needs to have accumulated enough voting power to meet the threshold required for creating new proposals.

To get the required voting power to create a proposal, you need at least 300,000 vpLSK.

There are two ways to get vpLSK:

  1. Stake your LSK for a specific amount of time. The tokens will be locked for the specified time, and you will receive vpLSK in return, which you can delegate to yourself to actively participate in governance.
  2. Get delegations from other users. Users who do not wish to actively participate in governance, can delegate their voting power to other users.
