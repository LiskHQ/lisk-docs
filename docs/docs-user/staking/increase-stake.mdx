This guide explains ste-by-step how to increase the amount of LSK staked in a particular [locking position](overview#locking-positions).

1. Go to your [Staking Dashboard](https://portal.lisk.com/staking/dashboard) in the Lisk Portal.
    Connect your wallet if you haven't already.
2. In the list of your locking positions, click on the locking position you wish to increase, to expand it.
    <img src={positions} /><br />
3. Click on the `Increase Stake` button.
4. Set the amount of LSK you want to add to the locking position.
    <img src={increaseApprove} /><br />
    Click on the `Approve` button to confirm the spending cap.
4. Confirm the action in your wallet.
5. Once the spending cap is approved, click the `Increase Stake` button.
    <img src={increase} /><br />
6. Confirm the action in your wallet.
7. Your locking position will now be increased by the specified amount.
    <img src={increased} /><br />

If your reload the page, you should see the increased stake of the locking position in the staking dashboard.
