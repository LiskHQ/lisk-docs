# On-Ramps

On-ramps (fiat-to-crypto) and off-ramps (crypto-to-fiat) are services that act as gateways between traditional fiat currencies (like USD or EUR) and the world of digital assets. 
They allow users to convert their local money into cryptocurrencies and vice versa through familiar payment methods such as bank transfers, credit cards, or Apple Pay.

The following on- and off-ramp service providers already support the Lisk network:

## Banxa

[Banxa](https://banxa.com/) is a global on/off-ramp integrating LSK, USDT, and USDC on Lisk Chain. 
Banxa provides easy, seamless integration of crypto payment services into any application by offering well-documented APIs and SDKs.

## MoonPay

[MoonPay](https://www.moonpay.com/) is a leading on/off-ramp provider and integrates LSK on Lisk Chain. 
With the MoonPay fiat-to-crypto on-ramp, users can seamlessly purchase cryptocurrencies directly from your website or web3 application.

## Fonbnk

[Fonbnk](https://www.fonbnk.com/) provides on- and off-ramps for USDT on Lisk Chain across Africa.

Fonbnk pairs prepaid mobile payments with stablecoins to eliminate fraud, reduce FX costs, and deliver real-time, chargeback-free global settlements.

## Kotani Pay

[Kotani Pay](https://kotanipay.com/) is a payment platform that provides a fiat-to-stablecoin on-ramp and off-ramp to local payment channels in Africa. 
Kotani Pay allows blockchain protocols, dApps, and blockchain fintechs to easily integrate payment solutions into their applications, websites, or web plugins. Kotani Pay integrates USDT and USDC on Lisk Chain.

## OnRamp Money

[OnRamp Money](https://onramp.money/) is an emerging-market-focused on/off-ramp with many local payment methods; it integrates LSK, USDT, and USDC on Lisk Chain.

## Noblocks

[Noblocks](https://www.noblocks.xyz/) is an intuitive interface that enables users to create payment orders for instant stablecoin-fiat exchanges. 
Noblocks integrates USDT on Lisk Chain. 
Built on Paycrest's decentralized liquidity network, it supports mobile money and bank settlements across multiple regions, including Kenya (KES), Uganda (UGX), Nigeria (NGN), Tanzania (TZS), and more.

## Aliniex

[Alix Pay](https://alixpay.com/), a core service of Aliniex, is designed to simplify crypto on- and off-ramp solutions for users worldwide. 
Alix Pay integrates LSK on Lisk Chain. Since its inception in 2017, Aliniex has established itself as a trusted cryptocurrency exchange, with a particularly strong presence in Southeast Asia.
